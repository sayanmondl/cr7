Cristiano's Iconic Bicycle Kick in 2018

Cristiano Ronaldo scored an insane bicycle goal in UCL 2018 Quarter final against Juventus. Cristiano himself said that this one is his favourite goal, although this did not get a Puskas award. Juventus defender Andrea Barzagli described this goal as "Playstation Goal". It garnered him a standing ovation from the opposing fans in the stadium as well as a plethora of plaudits from peers, pundits and coaches.

The SIUUUU... Celebration

After scoring a goal, he usually celebrates with a "storming jump" and "turn", before "landing in spread-eagled fashion" into his "signature power stance", while usually simultaneously exclaiming "Sí" It has also been known as "suiii", "siiuuu", "siii" or "Right Here Right Now", formally the Cristiano Ronaldo celebration.

17 Goals In a UCL Season!

By the end of the 2013-14 UEFA Champions League season, Ronaldo had broken the record for the most goals scored by a player in a single season, finding the back of the net 17 times to help Real Madrid win a record tenth title.
