Beyond the Football Pitch.
The Other Side of Cristiano Ronaldo.

Cristiano Ronaldo's influence reaches far beyond the football pitch. From business ventures to charity work, he continues to make an impact off the field, shaping industries and inspiring millions worldwide.

BUSINESS VENTURES

CR7 Brand

Ronaldo's personal brand, CR7, started with his signature clothing line. Over time, it has expanded into various product categories, including CR7 Eyewear, CR7 Underwear, CR7 Denim.

The CR7 brand has grown globally, symbolizing Ronaldo's influence in the fashion world.

CR7 Hotels (Pestana CR7)

In collaboration with the Pestana Hotel Group, Ronaldo has launched a series of luxury hotels under the Pestana CR7 name. These hotels are located in prime destinations such as: Lisbon and Funchal (Madeira), Ronaldo's hometown. Madrid and New York City, two cities with strong connections to his career.

CR7 Fitness

In partnership with Crunch Fitness, Ronaldo has invested in a chain of gyms known as CR7 Fitness. These gyms provide state-of-the-art fitness equipment and facilities, inspired by Ronaldo's own fitness regime. The goal is to make fitness more accessible, promoting a healthy lifestyle, and encouraging fans to stay fit like their idol.

Endorsements and Collaborations

Cristiano Ronaldo's global appeal has attracted numerous lucrative endorsement deals, with brands like Nike, Herbalife, and Clear. His lifetime deal with Nike is one of the most significant sponsorship contracts ever made with an athlete, further solidifying his status as a global brand ambassador.

HONORS AND TRIBUTES

CR7 Museum

The Museu CR7 is a museum that is dedicated to Cristiano Ronaldo's trophies. It is located in Ronaldo's birthplace, the city of Funchal on the Portuguese island of Madeira.

Cristiano Ronaldo International Airport

In a remarkable tribute to his global fame and contributions to the island of Madeira, the Madeira International Airport was renamed to Cristiano Ronaldo International Airport in 2017. This honor recognizes Ronaldo's achievements and the pride he brings to his home island.

PHILANTHROPY

Ambassador for Major Charities

Save the Children:    As an ambassador, Ronaldo has raised awareness and funds for the organization's efforts to support underprivileged children, especially in conflict and disaster zones.

UNICEF:    Ronaldo has long been involved with UNICEF, where he advocates for children's rights, education, and health.

World Vision:    Through his involvement with World Vision, Ronaldo has helped provide education, clean water, and healthcare for children in impoverished regions.

Golden Boot Auction for Gaza

In 2011, Ronaldo auctioned off his European Golden Boot award, raising €1.2 million to fund the construction of schools for children in Gaza. This highlighted his commitment to education and child welfare.

Disaster Relief Efforts

Ronaldo has shown compassion and support in times of natural disasters. In 2015, he donated a significant sum to help the victims of the Nepal earthquake and contributed to the rebuilding efforts.

In 2017, he auctioned off one of his Ballon d'Or trophies for £600,000, with the proceeds going to the Make-A-Wish Foundation, an organization that fulfills wishes for children facing critical illnesses.

Blood and Bone Marrow Donation Advocacy

Ronaldo is a regular blood donor and advocates for bone marrow donation, raising awareness about the importance of these life-saving practices. He leads by example, actively donating and encouraging others to do the same.

Fundraising through Football and Personal Donations

Ronaldo frequently auctions personal items, such as his football kits and memorabilia, to raise funds for charitable causes. His donations have supported efforts to combat child hunger and provide medical aid to communities in need.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Cristiano Ronaldo has a massive social media presence with over 1 billion followers across all platforms. His accounts are a mix of personal insights, promotions, endorsements, and sharing personal moments.
